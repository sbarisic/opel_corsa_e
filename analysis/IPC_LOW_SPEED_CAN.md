Interpretation: the primary and exact-table trials coincided with normal
IPC-origin chatter, but there was no clear new response or visible decoded
command effect in the captured data. Because the CANable may still be receiving
single-wire GMLAN-like traffic accidentally while failing to drive the physical
layer correctly, this is not a strong disproof of the BCM-derived frame content.
The firmware wake profile failure strengthens the physical-layer suspicion:
firmware-confirmed `0x100#` and `0x13FFE040#` did not visibly wake or affect the
cluster over the current adapter/wiring. The later firmware-shaped `0x621`
retry fixed the VNMF payload shape to match the observed IPC-origin `0x62C`
pattern, but the bench result was still only self-echo plus zero follow-up IPC
traffic.
